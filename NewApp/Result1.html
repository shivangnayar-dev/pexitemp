Your Temperament

Temperament is like the blueprint of who we are from the moment we're born. It shapes how we react to things and what we're naturally drawn to. Think of it as the foundation of your personality, influenced by both your genes and early experiences. But as we go through life, things like how we're raised, the culture we're part of, and the people we're around, all play a part in shaping and refining our temperament. Even though it changes over time, those core traits stick with us, guiding how we see the world and how we handle different situations. Understanding our temperament helps us understand ourselves better and grow as individuals.

Your Strengths (SWOT analysis)

Careers transcend mere jobs, encompassing behavioural, cognitive, people, and leadership skills alongside employability traits. Aligning with industry demands ensures success and fulfilment in chosen paths.

When selecting a career, consider four vital aspects:

  1. Behavioral Skills:Manage stress, encourage creativity, embrace continuous learning, uphold strong ethics, and maintain resilience.
  2. Cognitive Abilities:Analyse situations, reason logically, understand complexity, innovate solutions, and communicate effectively.
  3. People Skills:Build strong relationships, collaborate, and empathize with others.
  4. Leadership Skills:Inspire, motivate, and guide teams towards achieving goals.

Assess yourself across these dimensions to align with industry demands and maximize your potential for success and fulfilment.
